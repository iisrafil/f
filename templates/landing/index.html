About Us

Old Age Homes means an organisation which operates as an old age home used for the accommodation of retired people and which has applied for and been registered as old age home with the Department of Social Development, carries a certificate of registration as a Non-Profit Organisation and has inter alia indicated by affidavit (which need to be updated and submitted on an annual basis) how many people it houses. Like this, we are "Elders Care" an Old Aged Home organisation for our elderly people. We help our people who need a home to live his happy life. Here you can also help the elders people by donating us. You can check our website to visit the old aged homes. If you want to know more do contact with us.
